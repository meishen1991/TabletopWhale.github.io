About Tabletop Whale

The Author

Hi! My name's Eleanor Lutz and I write this blog. I'm a designer in Seattle and I have a Bachelor's in molecular biology from the University of Washington. I used to work in a research lab teaching mosquitoes to fly through mazes.

I spend my free time cooking, reading, playing computer games, and dabbling in frontend web design. Someday I want to write a science fiction book and visit Iceland.

Infographic Design

I make my infographics in Photoshop and Illustrator, usually with a Wacom drawing tablet. They're all based on reputable scientific sources like textbooks and peer-reviewed research papers. The website is a responsive Jekyll theme built from scratch and hosted on Github Pages.
